New Registration Process

This year we are managing the Art Festival with a NEW process.

At the bottom of this page is a link Continue Registrations. Once you click that link you will go to a page and see Arts Festival 2010 Registrations in a list of registrations. To the right is a button you need to click, Register. We probably already have a login and password for you.

If you are not sure of your login information click Forgot Your Password?, enter the e-mail address you have on file with PCPC and your login information will be e-mailed to you. If you get the box below click cancel and fill out the form on that page. We will e-mail you with how to send us your picture, signed agreement, and 100 word description that needs to be completed before you are officially entered into the Art Festival.

Thanks for being part of Park Cities Presbyterian Church 4th annual Art Festival. We are looking forward to many exciting entries and making this the best Art Festival Yet!
